Chocolate Granola Bars

Get Ready to Have Chocolate Fun!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Cups of Chocolate Chips
  • 4 Cups of Granola

Let's Start!

  1. Melt the Chocolate So It is Completley Liquid
  2. Mix the Chocolate & Granola Together Into One Bowl
  3. Shape into Rectangles
  4. Freeze for 2 Hours
  5. Cut, Eat, Enjoy
