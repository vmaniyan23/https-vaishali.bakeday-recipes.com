Pizza

Great for Dinner!

Ingredients:

  • Ready-Made Pizza Dough
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of Your Choice

Let's Start!

  1. Put the Dough Into a Pizza Pan & Shape It.
  2. Add the Tomatoe Sauce
  3. Add the Cheese On Top
  4. Add Your Toppings
  5. Bake in Oven at 350 Farenheit for 30 Minutes
  6. Cut, Serve, Enjoy!
